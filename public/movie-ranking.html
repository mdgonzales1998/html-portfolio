The Best Movies According to Michael Gonzales

My top 3 movies of all time

Dune Part 2

Recently saw this movie and was absolutely fantastic. Love the sci-fi, the characters, the pacing, the action, all of it is just so compelling and gripping. I can definetly tell how modern sci-fi and Star Wars were inspired by Dune

Tron Legacy

Pure nostalgia pick here. Love the super tech vibes, the story is also fairly good. Acting is wonderful and the action is very fun.

Demon Slayer: Mugen Train

The animation of this movie is absolutely insane. Ufotable highly elevated the source material and made the show super compelling and just beautiful. Demon Slayer in general has become a new standard for shonen action animation.
